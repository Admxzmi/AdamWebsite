ABOUT ME

Biography

FAMILY

My family consists of five members - myself, my sister, my brother, and our parents. Hi, the name's Noor Adam Bin Noor Azmi. 21 years of age. I am the youngest member of the family, with my siblings being older than me. We reside in a lovely sub-urban area called Kampung Sungai Kandis. Our house is a classic old Malay house, resembling the generation house of P. Ramlee. Despite our hectic schedules, we make time to spend quality time together and make memories that will last a lifetime. We always prioritise spending time with one together, whether it's a family dinner or a getaway for the weekend. As the youngest, I am frequently the centre of attention, and I appreciate the continual attention and affection shown to me by my family members. my family may be small, but the bond that we share is unbreakable. Our classic old Malay house may be traditional, but the love that we have for each other is modern and unending.

HOBBY

Eating is one of my greatest past times, and I adore experimenting with different cuisines. I am constantly looking for unique and intriguing flavour, whether it be in fine dining or street food. As long as the meal is worth the trip, I don't mind travelling far to test out new restaurants or eateries. My passion has taught me a lot about food culture over the years, and I now have a renewed respect for the art of cooking. I like trying out new dishes in my own kitchen, learning about the ingredients, and discovering various cuisines. But recently, my love of eating has also caused me to put on a few more pounds, and I am now battling to shed those pounds and return to my former ideal weight. But despite these difficulties, I still have a deep enthusiasm for food, and I like to try different flavours and foods. To sum up, I adore eating and consider it to be more than just a hobby. Despite the difficulties my food addiction may cause, I wouldn't give my passion for food for anything in the world. It has given me a great deal of delight and taught me about various cultures and flavours.

AMBITION

My goal is to work as a software developer, which is why I decided to enrol in a Science Computer course at UiTM Shah Alam. Technology fascinates me since it continues to change our lives and make everything easier to access and more effective. Given the reliance on technology in today's generation, I think that pursuing a career in software development gives me better chances to get employment quickly. Additionally, software development is a lucrative career choice due to the growing need for the profession. I think that if I am successful in accomplishing my goal, it will result in a comfortable life with stable finances for me. I hope to become a software developer so that I can start saving money so that I can marry my future spouse and have a happy marriage. In order to accomplish my own objectives, I'm thrilled to put my knowledge and experience to use in the business to support innovation and progress.
